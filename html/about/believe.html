What We Believe In

Here is a short info about what we beleive
in. And how God helps us.

The statement encapsulates the fundamental beliefs and practices of a Christian community. It affirms faith in Jesus Christ as Savior, the
authority of the Bible, and the Trinitarian concept of God. It acknowledges humanity's creation in holiness, subsequent fall into sin, and
the necessity of salvation through God's grace. The community emphasizes the offer of salvation to all through repentance and faith,
guided by the Holy Spirit. Belief in God's sovereignty in salvation and sanctification is upheld, alongside the continuous presence of
spiritual gifts and fruits. The local church is defined as a fellowship of baptized believers, engaging in practices such as baptism by
immersion and the Lord's Supper. Additionally, members are encouraged to practice tithing and voluntary giving to support the church's
mission. The observance of Sunday as the Lord's Day for worship is emphasized, as is submission to civil authorities in accordance with biblical
principles. There is a clear distinction between the saved and unsaved, and an anticipation of Christ's return and final judgment.
The community also acknowledges divine healing while affirming the use of medical treatment when necessary.
